Commission structure

Earn up to 5 levels deep.

Your referrals refer others, who refer more. You earn a percentage from all of them — up to 5 generations deep in your network.

Level Who they are Your commission
1 People you referred directly 50% of ride commission
2 People your referrals referred 20%
3 3rd-generation referrals 13%
4 4th-generation referrals 10%
5 5th-generation referrals 7%

What counts as a commission? A2B Lift takes a platform fee from every completed ride. Your referral commission is calculated as a percentage of that fee — not a percentage of what the driver or rider pays.
