What you actually earn

A simple 2.5% model.

A2B Lift does not use multi-level commissions. Rewards come from your own completed rides and the completed rides of riders you directly invited.

2.5% on your own rides

Every completed trip you take earns 2.5% back into your A2B Lift rewards wallet automatically.

2.5% on invited rider trips

When riders you directly referred complete trips, you earn another 2.5% from those completed rides.

Withdraw from R100

Once your rewards balance reaches R100, you can request a cash-out to your bank account from the app.

What counts as a commission? A2B Lift takes a platform fee from every completed ride. Your referral commission is calculated as a percentage of that fee — not a percentage of what the driver or rider pays.

Driver referrals

Earn when the drivers
you bring in keep moving.

1

Share your code

Your referral code works for both riders and drivers. Share it with any driver you know who's looking for flexible, well-paid chauffeur work.

2

They register & get approved

The driver signs up using your code, completes vehicle verification, and is approved on the platform. They're now linked to your referral.

3

You earn on their activity

Every trip your referred driver completes earns you 2.5% in loyalty rewards. The more they drive, the more you earn — passively.

2.5% on every referred driver trip

When a driver you referred completes a trip, 2.5% of the platform commission from that trip goes into your loyalty balance. Automatically, every time.

Stack with rider rewards

As a driver, you also earn 2.5% loyalty rewards on your own completed trips. Driver referrals and ride rewards both flow into the same wallet balance.

Withdraw from R100

Once your loyalty balance hits R100, request a cash-out to your bank account from within the driver app. No waiting period, no hidden fees.
