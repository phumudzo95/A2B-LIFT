For riders

A2B Lift is a premium ride-hailing and long-distance travel app available on Android and iOS. You can book on-demand rides in your city, or search for drivers posting inter-city routes.

How to book a ride

  1. Download A2B Lift from the App Store or Google Play.
  2. Create an account or sign in with Google.
  3. Enter your pickup location and destination.
  4. Confirm the fare and your chauffeur is matched instantly.
  5. Track your driver in real time and pay in-app.
Tip: Your account works on both the app and the A2B Lift website. No separate login needed.

For drivers

Register as an A2B Lift chauffeur to start earning. You choose when you drive and which rides you accept. There is no registration fee.

Requirements

  • Valid South African Professional Driving Permit (PrDP)
  • South African ID or passport
  • Roadworthy vehicle (model year 2015 or newer preferred)
  • Vehicle registration papers and insurance
  • Smartphone running Android 8+ or iOS 14+

Registration steps

  1. Complete the driver registration form — takes about 5 minutes.
  2. Upload your documents (ID, licence, vehicle papers).
  3. Our team reviews your application within 48 hours.
  4. Once approved, activate your account and start accepting rides.

Account & login

You can create an account with your email and phone number, or sign in with Google. One account gives you access to both the app and the web.

Forgot your password?

Use the "Forgot password" link on the login screen to receive a reset link by email.

Changing your phone number

Contact our support team to change your registered phone number. We'll verify your identity before making the change.

On-demand rides

On-demand rides connect you with a nearby verified chauffeur. The fare is calculated based on distance, time, and current demand. You'll see the price before you confirm.

Long distance travel

The A2B Lift long-distance marketplace lets drivers post inter-city routes and passengers book seats. It works differently from on-demand rides.

How it works for passengers

  1. Search your departure city and destination on the website or app.
  2. Browse available drivers, their vehicle, rating, and price per seat.
  3. Book and pay for your seat. Payment is held until the trip is completed.
  4. Meet your driver at the agreed pickup point on departure day.

How it works for drivers

  1. Post a route you're already planning to drive.
  2. Set the number of available seats and price per seat.
  3. Passengers book and pay. You receive a summary before departure.
  4. Complete the trip. Earnings are released to your wallet within 24 hours.
Note: Drivers post routes in advance. You do not respond to live pings for long-distance trips.

Booking in advance

You can pre-book an on-demand ride up to 90 days in advance. Useful for airport transfers, meetings, or any trip where timing matters.

To book in advance, tap "Schedule" when entering your destination in the app.

Cancellations

On-demand rides

You can cancel an on-demand ride for free within 2 minutes of booking. After that, a small cancellation fee may apply if the driver has already been dispatched.

Long-distance trips

Driver earnings

You keep the majority of every fare. A2B Lift's service fee is deducted automatically before earnings are credited to your wallet.

When do I get paid?

Earnings are credited to your in-app wallet after each completed trip. You can request a payout to your bank account at any time, subject to a minimum withdrawal amount.

Wallet & payouts

Your A2B Lift wallet holds your earnings and referral rewards. To withdraw:

  1. Open your wallet in the app.
  2. Tap "Withdraw" and enter the amount.
  3. Confirm your bank account details.
  4. Funds arrive within 1–2 business days.
Minimum withdrawal: R100

Referral programme

Earn 2.5% of every fare completed by a driver you referred to A2B Lift. There is no cap and no expiry date — as long as your referred driver is active, you earn.

How to refer a driver

  1. Open the app and go to Referrals.
  2. Share your unique referral link via WhatsApp, SMS, or any other channel.
  3. When a driver registers using your link and completes their first trip, you start earning 2.5% of every fare they earn.

Referral rewards for riders

Riders also earn rewards when they refer new passengers. Check the Referrals tab in the app for your current balance and reward history.

Referral links work on both the app and the website. A user who signs up via your referral link on the website is automatically linked to your account.

Driver verification

Every A2B Lift chauffeur is verified before they can accept rides. We check:

  • South African ID or valid passport
  • Professional Driving Permit (PrDP)
  • Criminal background record
  • Vehicle roadworthiness certificate
  • Vehicle insurance

Verification typically takes 24–48 hours after all documents have been submitted.

SOS & emergency

Your safety is our top priority. Every trip includes:

  • Live GPS tracking visible to you and your trusted contacts
  • In-app SOS button that alerts our safety team and sends your location
  • Trip sharing — send a live link to anyone so they can follow your journey

To use the SOS button, tap the shield icon during an active trip.

Corporate accounts

A2B Lift for Business lets companies manage travel for employees and clients from a single billing account.

Features include centralised invoicing, trip reports, spending limits per employee, and priority support.

To set up a corporate account, contact us at business@a2blift.com.

Data we collect

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Some features of the website may not function correctly without cookies enabled.
