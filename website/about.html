About A2B Lift

Built for how South Africa actually moves.

A2B Lift brings together premium on-demand rides, airport transfers, long-distance routes, and a real earning opportunity for drivers in one connected platform.

Why we exist

Travel should feel reliable, not uncertain.

Too many transport choices force people to compromise between comfort, trust, price clarity, and reach. A2B Lift was designed to close that gap. Riders can move through cities, to airports, and between provinces without juggling different services. Drivers can earn from real trip demand, route planning, and referrals inside the same ecosystem.

  • One account across app and website
  • On-demand, airport, and long-distance travel in one platform
  • Clear driver growth path through rides, routes, and referrals

What makes A2B Lift different

A wider travel network,
not just another ride button.

Premium city rides

Professional chauffeurs, real-time tracking, and a cleaner rider experience for daily travel.

Long-distance marketplace

Drivers can publish inter-city routes and passengers can search live availability instead of hoping for a generic quote.

Airport and corporate layers

Business and flight-related journeys have dedicated flows instead of being squeezed into a standard trip request.

Referral-powered growth

Riders and drivers can both earn from the network they build, with deeper commission structures for long-term participation.

Safety and trust

Growth only matters if riders feel secure.

Verification, driver accountability, route transparency, and support responsiveness are core to how we build. A2B Lift is not trying to be the biggest transport name on paper. We are building a platform that can be trusted across everyday rides and higher-stakes journeys like airport pickups and inter-city travel.
