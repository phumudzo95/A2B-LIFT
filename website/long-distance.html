Booking flow

manage it in your dashboard.

Live drivers show automatically

When drivers mark themselves available for long-distance trips in the app, their posted routes load here automatically as soon as the page opens.

Location search uses autosuggest

Type your departure city and destination and pick from location suggestions, just like the airport transfer page map flow.

Login keeps the booking tied to your dashboard

If you are not signed in, A2B Lift saves your trip intent first so you can continue after login and keep the confirmed booking visible in your dashboard.

Drivers get notified, and you choose payment

Each confirmed booking sends an in-app notification to the driver. Passengers can confirm with card online or select cash for the travel day.
