Referral programme

Riders and drivers
earn differently.

STEP 01

Share your referral code

Every A2B Lift account gets a referral code. Share it with riders and drivers. When they sign up and complete trips, your loyalty rewards grow automatically.

STEP 02

Referrals complete trips

When someone you referred — rider or driver — completes a trip on A2B Lift, you earn 2.5% in loyalty rewards from that trip. You also earn 2.5% on your own rides, every time.

STEP 03

Withdraw to your bank

Referral rewards and ride cashback both land in your A2B Lift wallet. Once your balance reaches R100, you can request a cash-out to your bank account directly from the app.

Rider referral rewards

Every trip you take earns 2.5% back as loyalty rewards. Every trip your referred rider completes earns you another 2.5%. Both flow into the same wallet automatically.

Driver referral rewards

When a driver you referred completes trips, you earn 2.5% of the platform commission from their activity. Stack this with your own trip rewards in one wallet.

One wallet, one payout flow

Whether your earnings come from rides, long-distance seats, rider referrals, or driver referrals, they are tracked in one place and move into the same wallet flow.
