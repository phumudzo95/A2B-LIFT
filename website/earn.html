Referral programme

Riders and drivers
earn differently.

STEP 01

Share one code, open two earning paths

Every account gets a referral code. Riders use it to build 5 levels of ride-based commission, while drivers can use it for direct driver referrals and IBO growth.

STEP 02

Riders and drivers trigger different rewards

Rider referrals earn 50%, 20%, 13%, 10%, and 7% across 5 levels of platform commission. Driver referrals earn 2.5% on direct driver activity, with optional matrix upside.

STEP 03

Payouts settle into your wallet

Referral commissions are tracked automatically and paid into your A2B Lift wallet. From there, you can spend them on trips or withdraw according to your account setup.

Rider referral plan

Level 1 pays 50%, then 20%, 13%, 10%, and 7% through level 5 of the platform commission from completed rides in your network.

Driver referral plan

Directly referred drivers pay you 2.5% of platform commission on their trips. Drivers who upgrade into the IBO system can also benefit from matrix placement and network point growth.

One wallet, one payout flow

Whether your earnings come from rides, long-distance seats, rider referrals, or driver referrals, they are tracked in one place and move into the same wallet flow.
