Safety

Safety isn't a feature.
It's the standard.

Every ride on A2B Lift is built around your safety — from the driver's first verification to the moment you arrive.

Driver verification

No unverified driver
ever gets through.

Before any driver can accept their first trip on A2B Lift, they go through a comprehensive multi-step verification process that we review manually.

  • South African Police Service (SAPS) criminal background check
  • Professional Driving Permit (PrDP) verification
  • Vehicle roadworthiness and licence disc check
  • Real-time selfie liveness check before every shift
  • Ongoing rating monitoring — drivers below threshold are suspended

While you ride

Tools that protect you
every step of the way.

Live trip tracking

Share your real-time route with family or friends directly from the app. They see every turn until you arrive.

In-app SOS

One tap sends your live GPS location, driver details, and trip information to your pre-set emergency contacts and our 24/7 safety team.

Verified driver identity

Check your driver's name, photo, vehicle, and plate number before you get in. If anything doesn't match, don't board.

Anonymous contact

Need to contact your driver? All calls and messages go through an anonymised relay — your personal number is never shared.

Two-way ratings

Passengers rate drivers and drivers rate passengers after every trip. Low-rated accounts on either side are reviewed automatically.

24/7 safety support

Our in-app safety team is available around the clock. Report any concern and get a response — not a chatbot.

Before every shift

Real-time liveness
selfie check.

Before a driver can go online, they complete a real-time selfie check. Our system confirms that the person behind the wheel matches the verified profile on file. No pass, no trips.

  • Takes less than 10 seconds for the driver
  • Flags mismatches and blocks access automatically
  • Reviewed by our safety team for any flagged cases
