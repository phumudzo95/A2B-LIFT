5 tips for booking a long-distance trip on A2B Lift

Everything you need to know before booking your next inter-city journey. Get the best driver, the best price, and arrive safely.

Booking a long-distance ride on A2B Lift is a fundamentally different experience from hailing an on-demand car. You're planning ahead. You're choosing from multiple drivers. You're paying for a seat, not the whole vehicle. And you're going to spend several hours in that car — so it pays to choose well.

Here are five things that experienced A2B Lift passengers do every time they book a long-distance trip.

1

Search early — availability fills up fast on popular routes

The Johannesburg to Durban and Johannesburg to Cape Town corridors are consistently the most searched routes on A2B Lift. On weekends and around public holidays, seats on quality drivers fill up days — sometimes a week — in advance. If you have a fixed travel date, search as soon as you know it. Booking early also gives you the widest choice of drivers and the best prices, since some drivers offer early-bird pricing to fill seats sooner.

2

Read the driver profile and check their rating carefully

Every driver on A2B Lift has a public profile showing their name, profile photo, vehicle details, star rating, and the number of completed trips. For long-distance travel, look for drivers with at least 4.7 stars and more than 20 completed trips. Read their recent reviews — passengers specifically mention things like comfort, cleanliness, punctuality, and how the driver handles long stretches of highway. A driver with 4.9 stars and 150 trips has earned that rating the hard way.

3

Confirm departure details and pickup location before the day

Unlike on-demand rides where a driver picks you up at your door, long-distance trips often have a fixed departure point that the driver has arranged in advance — typically a well-known landmark, fuel station, or central location in the city. The A2B Lift app shows the departure point when you view a driver's route. If anything is unclear, use the in-app chat to message your driver and confirm exact meeting details before travel day. Do this the evening before, not 10 minutes before departure.

4

Share your trip link with someone you trust

This is not optional — it is part of travelling safely. Once your booking is confirmed, the A2B Lift app generates a live trip tracking link. Send this to a family member or trusted friend. The link shows your driver's details, your route, your estimated arrival time, and updates in real time. It takes 10 seconds and gives both you and your loved ones peace of mind for the entire journey. A2B Lift also monitors active long-distance trips and will follow up automatically if a trip runs significantly over its expected duration.

5

Use your wallet balance to book — it's faster and earns you rewards

A2B Lift users who book long-distance trips using their in-app wallet balance earn reward points on the transaction. You also skip the card payment step, which means booking is confirmed instantly with no waiting for payment processing. To top up your wallet, go to your A2B Lift dashboard, tap "Wallet," and add funds using Paystack — South Africa's most trusted payment platform. Wallet top-ups are instantaneous and fully secure.

What to bring on a long-distance trip

Practical preparation makes any long journey better:

  • Headphones. Even if your driver is great company, you may want to sleep or work. A good pair of noise-cancelling headphones is the single best long-distance travel accessory.
  • Snacks and water. Most A2B Lift long-distance trips include at least one rest stop, but the timing and location depend on the driver and the route. Don't depend on a service station being open when you need it.
  • A phone charger. Your phone is your booking confirmation, your trip tracker, and your emergency contact. Keep it charged. Most A2B Lift vehicles have a USB port — confirm with your driver if in doubt.
  • A neck pillow. If you're travelling more than 4 hours, this is not optional. Your future self will thank your current self.
  • Cash for tolls. Some routes pass through toll plazas that charge passengers. Your driver will let you know in advance — it is standard practice to split tolls evenly between all passengers.

What if my driver cancels?

Driver cancellations are rare on A2B Lift — the platform's acceptance and completion rate tracking means drivers with a pattern of cancellations are reviewed and can be removed from long-distance listings. If a driver does cancel, you receive a full refund to your original payment method or wallet balance within 24 hours. The platform will also surface alternative drivers on the same route and date if available.
