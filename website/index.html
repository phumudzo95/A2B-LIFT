Our safety standards
Background-checked drivers
Every chauffeur passes a criminal background check and professional driver licence verification before being approved on the platform.
Live trip tracking
Every trip is tracked in real time. Share your live location with trusted contacts, and your emergency number is one tap away at all times.
Identity verified riders
Clients verify their identity with a selfie during registration. Drivers can see who is requesting before they accept, creating mutual accountability.
Vehicle roadworthiness
All vehicles are checked for a valid roadworthy certificate before the driver can go online. Minimum vehicle standards are enforced by category.
